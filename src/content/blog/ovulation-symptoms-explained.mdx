## Why people look for symptoms

Symptoms can feel more personal and immediate than a calendar estimate, especially for people tracking fertility closely.

## Common signs

- cervical mucus changes
- mild pelvic discomfort
- libido changes
- basal temperature shifts after ovulation

## Why symptoms are not enough alone

Symptoms vary from person to person, and some cycles provide clearer signals than others.
