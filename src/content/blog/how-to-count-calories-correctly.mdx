## Why counting calories fails for some people

Calorie counting is not only about effort. It is also about portion accuracy, consistency, and understanding what really counts.

## Common tracking mistakes

- logging raw food as cooked food
- skipping oils and condiments
- using generic database entries
- not tracking weekend intake
