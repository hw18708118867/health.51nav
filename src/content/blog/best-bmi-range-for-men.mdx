## Why this topic matters

Many men use BMI as a quick checkpoint when planning fat loss or general health goals. It is easy to calculate, but the number can be misleading if muscle mass is high.

## Healthy BMI range for men

The general healthy BMI range is still 18.5 to 24.9 for most adult men. The interpretation becomes less precise for lifters, athletes, and people with more muscular builds.

## Where men get tripped up

Common issues include:

- assuming a higher BMI always means excess fat
- ignoring waist size and body fat percentage
- chasing a number instead of a sustainable health range
