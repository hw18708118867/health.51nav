## Why there is no single perfect ratio

Fat loss depends on calorie balance first. Macro ratios matter because they affect fullness, training quality, and consistency.

## What usually works well

Most fat-loss setups work better when protein stays high, fat stays sufficient, and carbs fill the rest according to preference and activity.

## A practical approach

Use the [Macro Calculator](/macro-calculator) to build the plan, the [Protein Calculator](/protein-calculator) to protect lean mass, and the [Calorie Calculator](/calorie-calculator) to keep the deficit realistic.

## Common mistakes

- cutting protein too low
- lowering fats too aggressively
- assuming very low carbs are required
