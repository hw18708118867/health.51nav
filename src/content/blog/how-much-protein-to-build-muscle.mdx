## Why protein gets so much attention

Protein supports muscle repair, recovery, and long-term muscle gain. That is why it is often the first macro people set when planning a fitness diet.

## A practical target range

Many active adults do well around 1.6 to 2.2 grams of protein per kilogram of body weight per day, especially when training regularly. That range is usually high enough to support progress without turning every meal into a chore.

## When protein needs rise

Higher protein can be especially useful when:

- cutting calories
- trying to preserve lean mass
- training hard multiple times per week
