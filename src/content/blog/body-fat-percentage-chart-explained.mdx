## Why body fat charts are popular

People want a clear label for where they stand. Body fat charts can offer that structure, but the ranges are still estimates and vary by sex, age, and athletic background.

## Typical chart ranges

Charts usually separate essential fat, athletic, fit, average, and higher body fat ranges.

## How to use the chart well

Use the chart to understand trends, not to chase an exact label. A one-percent difference on paper often means less than consistency over time.
