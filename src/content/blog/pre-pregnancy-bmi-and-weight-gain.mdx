## Why pre-pregnancy BMI is used

Recommended pregnancy weight gain ranges are often based on pre-pregnancy BMI because starting body size influences how much gain is usually advised.

## What that means in practice

Different starting BMI categories are linked to different recommended total-gain ranges.
