## Why meal planning helps

Many people know their calorie target but struggle to apply it consistently. A meal plan turns a daily number into repeatable decisions.

## What good meal planning does

- reduces decision fatigue
- helps manage hunger
- makes protein and fiber easier to hit
- lowers the chance of random overeating
