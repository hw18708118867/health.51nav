## Why maintenance calories matter

Maintenance calories are the anchor point for both fat loss and muscle gain. If the maintenance estimate is too high or too low, every adjustment built on top of it can drift in the wrong direction.

## Start with an estimate

The easiest way is to use the [TDEE Calculator](/tdee-calculator), which starts with BMR and adds activity level.

## Then validate with tracking

Watch average body weight, energy levels, and intake over two to four weeks. If body weight holds fairly steady across that stretch, you are probably close to maintenance.

## Common reasons the estimate is wrong

- overestimating activity
- underreporting food intake
- reacting to daily scale noise

## Final takeaway

The first maintenance estimate is rarely the final one. Use the [TDEE Calculator](/tdee-calculator) to get close, then let tracking tell you whether the number fits real life well enough to keep.
