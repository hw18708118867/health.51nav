## Why this topic gets so much search traffic

People often know the calories in single foods, but meals are more confusing because portions, sauces, oils, and restaurant choices add variability.

## Where estimates go wrong

Common misses include:

- hidden oils
- beverages
- large restaurant portions
- not weighing calorie-dense extras
