## Why one number is rarely enough

An ideal weight formula gives a useful reference, but a realistic goal weight often depends on muscle mass, lifestyle, and what you can maintain.

## What ideal weight means

Ideal weight calculators use height-based formulas to estimate a general healthy target.

## What goal weight means

A goal weight is personal. It should reflect health, performance, energy, and sustainability rather than a formula alone.
