## Why accuracy is limited

BAC calculators use formulas, but the body does not process alcohol in a perfectly predictable way. Food intake, metabolism, medication use, body size, drink strength, and timing can all shift the result.

## What they are good for

They are most useful for showing direction. They help explain why body weight, drink count, and time spent drinking can push an estimate up or down.

## What they are not good for

They should never be used to decide whether it is safe to drive, work, supervise others, or make any high-stakes decision. A rough estimate is not the same as a measured result, and even a measured result does not guarantee unimpaired judgment.

## Final takeaway

BAC calculators are useful for education, not for clearance. Use the [BAC Calculator](/bac-calculator) to understand how risk rises, but treat the result as a warning tool rather than permission.
