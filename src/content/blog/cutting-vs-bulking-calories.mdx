## Why the calorie target changes with the goal

Cutting and bulking are built on opposite calorie setups. A cut requires a deficit, while a bulk requires a surplus.

## Cutting calories

Cutting usually works best with a moderate deficit that preserves training performance and muscle retention.

## Bulking calories

Bulking usually works best with a smaller surplus than most beginners expect. Overshooting calories too hard often adds unnecessary fat.
