## Why zone 2 is everywhere

Zone 2 is popular because it supports aerobic development without the recovery cost of harder interval work.

## What zone 2 generally feels like

It is usually a steady conversational pace where breathing is elevated but still controlled.

## Best way to estimate it

The [Heart Rate Zone Calculator](/heart-rate-zone-calculator) gives a useful numerical estimate, but perceived effort still matters.
