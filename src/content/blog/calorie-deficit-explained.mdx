## What a calorie deficit means

A calorie deficit happens when you consistently eat fewer calories than your body uses. Over time, that is what drives fat loss.

## Why the size of the deficit matters

Too small can feel frustratingly slow. Too large can hurt training, recovery, and adherence.

## A practical setup

Use the [TDEE Calculator](/tdee-calculator) to estimate maintenance, then the [Calorie Calculator](/calorie-calculator) to set a moderate deficit. The [Macro Calculator](/macro-calculator) helps structure the diet.

## Common misconceptions

- sweating more does not mean a larger fat-loss deficit
- skipping meals is not automatically better
- faster scale change is not always better progress
