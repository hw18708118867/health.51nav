## Why body weight is used in hydration formulas

Larger bodies usually need more fluids, which is why many hydration tools begin with body weight rather than a fixed universal rule.

## Where the formula helps

It gives a baseline that is more personalized than generic advice and easier to adapt for climate and exercise.

## Where it is not enough

Body weight alone cannot capture sweat rate, activity, illness, or environmental heat.
