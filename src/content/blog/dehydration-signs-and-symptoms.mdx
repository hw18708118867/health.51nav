## Why people miss mild dehydration

Early dehydration can feel like normal fatigue, reduced focus, or an off day. That is why simple symptom awareness matters.

## Common signs

- dark urine
- headache
- unusual fatigue
- dizziness
- poor workout performance
- strong thirst
