## Why this comparison matters

People often want to know whether they need to go low-carb to lose weight. In reality, diet adherence and total calories matter more than one popular label.

## Where low-carb can help

Lower-carb diets may help some people control hunger or simplify food choices.

## Where balanced diets shine

Balanced diets often support training performance, food variety, and easier social eating.
