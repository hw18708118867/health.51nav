## Why fat matters

Dietary fat supports hormones, nutrient absorption, brain function, and meal satisfaction. It should not be treated as something to minimize at all costs.

## A practical range

Many balanced diets fall around 20% to 35% of calories from fat, though body size and food preference also matter.

## How to set the number

Use the [Fat Intake Calculator](/fat-intake-calculator), then coordinate it with the [Macro Calculator](/macro-calculator) and [Calorie Calculator](/calorie-calculator).

## Common mistake

People often cut fat too low while focusing only on protein and calories. That can make the diet feel unsatisfying and harder to maintain.
