## Why food quality still matters

Even when calories and macros are the main targets, the source of dietary fat still affects nutrition quality and meal satisfaction.

## Strong fat sources to highlight

- olive oil
- avocados
- nuts and seeds
- fatty fish
- yogurt and eggs in balanced amounts
