## Why there is no universal number

There is no single drink number that guarantees a certain BAC. The answer depends on who is drinking, how quickly the drinks are consumed, how strong those drinks are, and what else is happening around them.

## Variables that change the answer

- drink size
- alcohol content
- body weight
- sex
- time

## Use the estimate carefully

The [BAC Calculator](/bac-calculator) can show how quickly estimates rise, but it should always be treated as educational rather than definitive.

## Why drink counting can be misleading

People often say they had "two drinks" or "three drinks" as if every drink were the same size. In real life, pours vary a lot. A large glass of wine, a strong mixed drink, and a standard beer do not always contain the same amount of alcohol.

That is why drink count by itself can create false confidence. Even before legal limits are involved, judgment, coordination, and reaction time may already be affected.

## Final takeaway

The number of drinks that raises BAC is different from one situation to the next. Use the [BAC Calculator](/bac-calculator) to see how quickly the estimate can climb, and assume more caution rather than less.
