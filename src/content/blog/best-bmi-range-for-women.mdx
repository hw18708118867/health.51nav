## Why this topic matters

Women often search for a healthy BMI range because they want a simple benchmark. BMI can provide that starting point, but it works best when it is used alongside context like age, muscle mass, and body fat percentage.

## Standard BMI range for women

For most adult women, the standard healthy BMI range is 18.5 to 24.9. That range is widely used in public health, but it is still a screening tool rather than a full health assessment.

## When BMI is less informative

BMI may miss important context for:

- strength-trained women
- postpartum women
- women with low muscle mass
- women comparing fitness progress rather than weight alone
