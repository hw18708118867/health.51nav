## Why lowering BMI should be done carefully

The goal is not to force weight down as fast as possible. The goal is to improve health markers and body composition in a way you can sustain.

## The basic path

BMI usually goes down when body weight goes down while height stays the same. In practical terms, that often means creating a modest calorie deficit and increasing daily movement.

## Safe habits that matter most

- set calories from the [Calorie Calculator](/calorie-calculator)
- estimate maintenance with the [TDEE Calculator](/tdee-calculator)
- favor a moderate deficit instead of crash dieting
- keep protein high to preserve lean mass

## What to avoid

Very low-calorie diets, dehydration tricks, and scale obsession can lower body weight temporarily without improving health.
