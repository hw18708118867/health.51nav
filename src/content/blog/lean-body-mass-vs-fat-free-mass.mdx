## Why these terms get confused

Lean body mass and fat-free mass are close concepts, so many people use them as if they mean exactly the same thing.

## The main difference

Fat-free mass removes all fat tissue. Lean body mass is often used more loosely and may include essential lipids depending on the context. In everyday fitness use, they are often treated similarly.

## Why it matters in practice

The bigger lesson is not the vocabulary. It is understanding how much of your body weight is not fat, because that helps with protein planning and progress tracking.
