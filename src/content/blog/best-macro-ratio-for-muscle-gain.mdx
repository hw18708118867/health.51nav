## Why muscle gain macros look different

Muscle gain diets still need enough total calories, but they also benefit from strong protein intake and enough carbohydrates to support training.

## General macro priorities

- protein stays reliably high
- carbs often rise to support performance
- fats stay moderate, not minimal

## Where to start

The [Macro Calculator](/macro-calculator) creates a balanced starting point, while the [Protein Calculator](/protein-calculator) and [Calorie Calculator](/calorie-calculator) help refine the plan.

## What to avoid

A large calorie surplus usually adds fat faster than it adds muscle. A controlled surplus is often more efficient.
