## Why carb needs vary so much

Carbohydrate needs change with activity level, training intensity, and the rest of the diet. There is no fixed number that works for everyone, which is why blanket carb advice often feels confusing.

## Who usually needs more carbs

People doing higher-volume training or more intense cardio often feel and perform better with more carbohydrates.

## Who may prefer fewer carbs

Less active adults may choose a lower-carb setup for appetite control or simplicity, as long as calories and protein still make sense.

## Why context matters more than carb fear

Carbs are often treated as either essential performance fuel or the reason everything went wrong. In reality, they are simply one part of the bigger diet picture.

Someone training hard several days a week will usually feel and perform differently from someone who is mostly sedentary. That difference matters more than whether carbs are fashionable in the moment.
