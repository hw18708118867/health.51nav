## Why this question matters

Many parents-to-be want one exact answer, but pregnancy dating always includes some uncertainty.

## What affects accuracy

- regularity of cycles
- known conception timing
- variation in ovulation
- ultrasound findings

## Best way to use the tool

Use the [Due Date Calculator](/due-date-calculator) as a starting estimate, then rely on medical guidance for the most precise dating available.
